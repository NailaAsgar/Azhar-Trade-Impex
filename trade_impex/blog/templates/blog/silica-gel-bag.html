Silica Gel Bag


We are also Manufacturer of White Silica Gel Bag & Blue Silica Gel Bag. Silica Gel Bags are made with the help of Non-Woven Cloth or Cotton Cloth & Silica Gel. Silica Gel Bags are also known as Container Desiccant, Desiccant Bag, Container Desiccant Bag, Silica Gel Desiccants Bag, Silica Gel Pouch, Silica Gel Packets. Silica Gel Bag manufactured by us are 100 % DMF Free.

  • Blue Silica Gel Bags (Indicating Type Silica Gel Bag)

  • 1-kg Silica Gel Bag
  • 1.5-kg Silica Gel Bag
  • 2-kg Silica Gel Bag


    • Silica Gel Bags Available with Us in different Hanging Option :

  • Hook Type
  • Hanger Type
  • Bag Type
  • Plane Bag

    Non-Woven Cloth, Cotton Cloth
